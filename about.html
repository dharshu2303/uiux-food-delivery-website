KK MESS

Welcome to KK Mess Restaurant, where every meal feels like home.

At KK Mess, we believe that food is more than just fuel — it’s a feeling. Our journey began with a simple dream: to serve fresh, home-cooked meals to students, working professionals, and families who miss the warmth of traditional South Indian cooking.

Our mission is to bring you authentic, hygienic, and tasty meals made with love, using the freshest ingredients and time-tested recipes. Whether you’re looking for a quick lunch, a hearty dinner, or a weekly subscription, KK Mess is your go-to destination for wholesome, mess-style food.

From traditional south dishes to north indian travelling over american dishes — we’ve got it all covered. Our meals are prepared in a clean, homely kitchen and delivered hot and fresh right to your doorstep, thanks to our easy-to-use food delivery platform.

We understand the fast-paced life of our customers and ensure timely delivery without compromising on taste or quality. With KK Mess, you don’t just eat — you experience the comfort of home in every bite.

Join the KK Mess family today and let us serve you meals that are simple, soulful, and satisfying — just like how Amma makes it.
